Friscora

Income, expenses, goals, and analytics—plus a work schedule planner built for real life.

Available on the App Store

Stay in control of your money

Friscora is a native SwiftUI app for iPhone: fast, private-first, and focused on clarity—not buzzwords.

📊 Dashboard & analytics

See your balance, monthly flow, and spending by category with charts tuned for quick decisions.

🗓 Work schedule & salary view

Plan shifts and patterns, navigate the calendar, and understand how work lines up with your finances.

🎯 Goals

Set savings targets and track progress alongside day-to-day expenses.

📄 Statement import

Bring in bank statements and categorize with a deterministic, rule-based engine—no in-app AI assistant.

🔗 Optional schedule sharing

Pair schedules securely when you choose to use sharing; backed by Firebase Auth and Firestore.

🌐 Multiple languages

English, Kazakh, Polish, and Russian string support for a broader audience.

Your data stays yours

Core finance and schedule data lives on your device. Optional iCloud Key-Value and Firebase-backed features are used only where the app clearly needs them—for example schedule sharing and feedback.

Built for privacy

Optional app lock, on-device statement handling, and a privacy manifest aligned with App Store expectations. Read the full policy for details.
